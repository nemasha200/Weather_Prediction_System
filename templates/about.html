About This Weather Prediction System

This weather dashboard is designed to provide accurate and timely weather predictions for farmers and local businesses. It integrates real-time weather data, advanced machine learning models, and IoT devices to help stakeholders make informed decisions.

Farmers can use this system to plan irrigation, fertilizer use, and harvesting schedules, while businesses can plan deliveries, inventory, and staffing. Our goal is to increase productivity, reduce waste, and help communities adapt to changing weather conditions.
